Game Introduction
游戏介绍

Seven Day Adventure is an interactive STEM themed learning journey. Students explore imaginative planets, solve science based challenges, and follow a story that connects creativity with real world concepts. Each day introduces a new world inspired by physics, mathematics, engineering, and inquiry based thinking.

七日冒险是一个互动式的 STEM 学习旅程。 学生将探索由想象力构建的星球，通过解决科学挑战来推动故事发展。 每一天都会带来受物理、数学、工程和探究式学习启发的新世界，让知识与创造力并行。
